NYSC Tutor Recruitment

Teach Maths online, earn consistently, and build proof of your skills.

We are recruiting NYSC members who can teach Maths confidently in a virtual environment. The process includes a short Maths test and a live teaching demonstration.

Apply as a Tutor

Selection steps

  1. Application and screening
  2. 2 part test: Maths proficiency, behavioural and values
  3. Live teaching demonstration (choose 1 of 3 topics)

Minimum requirements

1

NYSC status

You are currently serving or recently completed NYSC, and can provide proof if needed.

2

Maths competence

You can teach Maths clearly up to WAEC and GCSE level, and can pass a short standardised test.

3

Virtual engagement

You can keep students engaged online and explain steps clearly, ideally using a stylus or writing tool.

4

Reliable setup

Stable internet, a laptop, and a quiet space. A tablet with stylus is preferred.
